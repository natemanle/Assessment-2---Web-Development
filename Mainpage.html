What is Terraria?

Terraria is a land of adventure! A land of mystery! A land that's yours to shape, defend, and enjoy. Your options in Terraria are limitless. Are you an action-seeker with an itchy trigger finger? A master builder? A collector? An explorer? There's something for everyone.

Blends elements of classic action games with the freedom of sandbox-style creativity, Terraria is a unique gaming experience where both the journey and the destination are as unique as the players themselves!
