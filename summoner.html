Pre-Moon Lord

Summoning

The armor, accessories, and buffs/potions from the Pre-Lunatic Cultist section should be used in tandem to these.

Minions

Best
Stardust Dragon Staff
Terraprisma
Stardust Cell Staff

Whips

Constellation
Firecracker

Summoners have a choice between two main options. The first choice is the Stardust Dragon Staff, which performs well with the Constellation due to its consistent damage. The Firecracker can be substituted as a high-risk, high-reward means of damage, although it is less consistent than the Constellation. The other option is to use the Terraprisma, and its fast attack speed makes it work well with the Constellation due to its relatively high tag damage effect.

For endgame there are once again best whip-summon combinations. The Stardust Dragon Staff works excellently with the Constellation which combines consistent damage with its range and homing projectiles. Another option while using the Stardust Dragon Staff is to use the Firecracker due to its high base damage and Firecracker's effect multiplying the damage dealt. But due to the Firecracker's low range, it requires you to get close to enemies and bosses, although Tiki armor can help with this. The Terraprisma benefits from the Possession due to its brisk attack speed benefiting from the high tag damage from the Possession. However, to obtain one you must fight the Empress of Light during daytime, which is an incredibly difficult task as every attack from the boss will one-shot the player during the daytime.
